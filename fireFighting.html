Fire Fighting

ACE Projects is a multifaceted organization specializing in the installation and maintenance of a diverse range of fire protection systems. From hydrants and wet sprinklers to pumping and piping systems, pre-action systems, medium and high-velocity systems, hand appliances, and industrial piping, our expertise covers a comprehensive array of components essential for effective fire safety.

In the realm of fire protection, we excel in the swift design and implementation of hydrant systems, ensuring efficient water access during emergencies. Additionally, our proficiency extends to wet sprinkler systems designed for rapid response, as well as the installation and maintenance of pumping and piping systems for seamless water distribution. With tailored services in pre-action systems, medium and high-velocity systems, hand appliances, and industrial piping, ACE Projects stands as a reliable partner, utilizing our diverse skill set to deliver effective solutions crucial for safeguarding properties and lives.
