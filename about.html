About

ACE Projects has successfully established its presence in the market by adhering to the principles of "TRUST," "COMMITMENT," and "LEARNING." These core values form the foundation of our business, driving our strength and credibility. As a multi-disciplinary engineering contracting organization, we bring forth new ideas, innovations, and contribute to creating a dynamic market environment. Our comprehensive HVAC & MEP services cater to diverse sectors, including Commercial, Process & Industrial cooling, Data Centre Cooling, Healthcare, green field & brown field projects, and energy-efficient repair & retrofit assignments.

At ACE Projects, we prioritize the entire life cycle of a project, offering end-to-end solutions from inception to implementation. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and adherence to budgets has been instrumental in building our reputation. We leverage the latest technologies and methodologies, providing a flexible approach to contracting structures. Our team's expertise spans various mechanical and electrical systems, including

  • VRV/VRF systems
  • Chiller Plants(Screw, Centrifugal, Scroll, etc.)
  • OT/Clean Rooms/BSL Labs applications
  • DX/Chilled Water Systems
  • Industrial / Process Cooling
  • Operation & Maintenance Service Contracts
  • Energy Efficiency Solutions
  • Electrical Systems
  • Fire Fighting
  • Commercial Refrigeration
  • Energy Audits/monitoring/metering
  • BMS System

We place a strong emphasis on Occupational Health and Safety, Environmental Protection, and Quality Assurance, ensuring a healthy and safe work environment. Our dedicated professionals work tirelessly to exceed client expectations, delivering 100% satisfactory and quality services. Discover the difference with ACE Projects - contact us to explore how we can contribute to the success of your HVAC project execution needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading MEP contracting company, delivering innovative and sustainable solutions that empower our clients and communities to thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional MEP contracting services, driven by our commitment to safety, quality, innovation, and sustainability. We aim to exceed our clients’ expectations by delivering customized solutions that meet their unique needs and provide long-term value.
